Web Browser's Fingerprint

This website is built for a master's thesis work at University of Helsinki, department of Computer Science. It collects information anonymously from your browser for fingerprinting purpose. The information will be stored on our server and will be analyzed to measure the uniqueness of your browser.

When you click the button below your browser will ask your to grant permissions for the website to the following features (if supported):

  • Geolocation
  • Web-camera and microphone
  • Notification
  • MIDI devices

Please grant the permissions you feel comfortable granting to websites. We do not record neither video, nor audio. We do not send notifications or use MIDI devices. We ask for this permission to assess the avaliable features of your browser and how much information they disclose about you.
